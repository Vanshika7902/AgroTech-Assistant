Crop insurance schemes

  • Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
  • Weather based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)
  • Palm insurance scheme (CPIS) and
  • Pilot Unified Package insurance scheme UPIS) (45 districts).

Benefits

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Insurance protection for food crops, oilseeds and annual horticultural/commercial crops notified by state government. Uniform maximum premium for all farmers: 1.Kharif season - 2% of sum insured. 2.Rabi Season 1.5% of sum insured. 3.Annual commercial/horticultural crops - 5% of sum insured

Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)

Insurance protection for notified food crops, oilseeds and horticultural /commercial crops Uniform maximum premium for all farmers like PMFBY : 1.Kharif season - 2% of sum insured. 2.Rabi Season 1.5% of sum insured. 3.Commercial/horticultural crops 5% of sum insured

Coconut Palm Insurance Scheme (CPIS)

1.Insurance protection for Coconut Palm growers. 2.Premium rate per palm ranges from Rs. 9.00 (in the plant age group of 4 to 15 years) to Rs. 14.00 (in the plant age group of 16-60 years). 3.50-75% subsidy of premium is provided to all types of farmers. 4.When the palm damaged, the claim payment equal to input cost loss damage is payable to the insured in notified areas.
