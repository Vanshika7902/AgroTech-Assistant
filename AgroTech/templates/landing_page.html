About Us

Agrotech Assistant is an online platform that helps farmers improve their yields and profitability. Our platform provides various details about different fields in agriculture to ease the work of farmers. Sign up today to join our community!

Our facilities include:

  • Real-time weather updates for your location
  • Information about government schemes and subsidies
  • Tools for buying and selling crops directly with buyers and other farmers

Facilities

Weather

Our website provides real-time weather and climate details for any area to assist farmers.Access to up-to-date information on temperature, rainfall, humidity, wind speed, & weather patterns can help farmers make decisions related to long-term planning, such as selecting crops that are more resilient to weather.
Transport

Our website provides a transport facility to farmers for transportation of their produce to the market.In remote areas where transportation can be a challenge, it ensures their products reach market safely & efficiently. It reduces the hassle for farmer of finding transportation themselves.
Government Schemes

Our website provides information about various government schemes that are available for farmers. This includes details about the eligibility criteria, application process, and benefits of the schemes. It is easily accessible to farmers so they can take advantage of the schemes and improve their livelihoods.
