About Us

Agrotech Assistant is an online platform that helps farmers improve their yields and profitability. Our platform provides access to the latest weather information, government schemes, and tools for buying and selling crops.

Our facilities include:

  • Real-time weather updates for your location
  • Information about government schemes and subsidies
  • Tools for buying and selling crops directly with buyers and other farmers
